Our Story

Built on the belief that everyone deserves financial confidence

Brightside Finance Foundation is a comprehensive initiative designed to empower students and adults with essential financial literacy skills. By providing accessible tools, interactive workshops, and tailored resources, Brightside equips individuals to confidently manage budgets, navigate financial challenges, and build a strong foundation for long-term financial independence.

// Our Journey

How we got here

August 2024 - Founded
An idea becomes a reality

Co-founders Shashank Kamineni, Vijay Venkatesan, and Samuel Deepak launch Aspire Finance with one common goal in mind: to address the declining financial literacy rate in Texas.

October 2024 - First Seminars
Initial Seminars at Reedy High School

After finalizing the curriculum, Aspire Finance hosts its first seminars at Reedy High School, bringing financial literacy education directly to the community.

December 2024 - Creation of Products
Aspire Finance broadens its horizons

The team develops a suite of products for the community to enjoy. This includes a board game, a mobile app, and a website.

March 2025 - State Finalist
Reaching a State-wide Audience

Aspire Finance earns state-level recognition at the Texas DECA State Career Development Conference, validating its mission and impact across Texas.

January 2026 - Brightside Finance Rebrand
New Light; Same Vision.

The team relaunched under the Brightside Finance brand to pursue a broader vision of Financial Literacy while expanding its workforce significantly.
